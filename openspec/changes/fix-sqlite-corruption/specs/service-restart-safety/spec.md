## ADDED Requirements

### Requirement: macOS LaunchAgent has ThrottleInterval
The LaunchAgent plist generated by service-installer.ts SHALL include ThrottleInterval to prevent crash loops.

#### Scenario: LaunchAgent plist includes ThrottleInterval
- **WHEN** service-installer.ts generates the macOS plist
- **THEN** the plist includes `<key>ThrottleInterval</key><integer>30</integer>`
- **AND** this ensures at least 30 seconds between restart attempts

#### Scenario: Crash loop prevented on macOS
- **WHEN** daemon crashes repeatedly (e.g., due to corrupt DB before recovery)
- **THEN** launchd waits 30 seconds between restart attempts
- **AND** system resources are not exhausted by rapid restarts

### Requirement: systemd unit has restart limits
The systemd unit generated by service-installer.ts SHALL include StartLimitBurst and StartLimitIntervalSec.

#### Scenario: systemd unit includes restart limits
- **WHEN** service-installer.ts generates the systemd unit
- **THEN** the unit includes:
  - `StartLimitBurst=5`
  - `StartLimitIntervalSec=600`
- **AND** RestartSec=2 is retained for normal restarts

#### Scenario: Crash loop prevented on Linux
- **WHEN** daemon crashes more than 5 times in 10 minutes
- **THEN** systemd stops attempting restarts
- **AND** logs indicate the service has failed too many times
- **AND** manual intervention is required to restart
